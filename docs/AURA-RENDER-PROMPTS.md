## ★ BLOCK 1 — DESIGN DNA (paste at the top of EVERY render)

```
STYLE & ART DIRECTION — reuse for every section:
A premium, editorial website design concept for "aro" — a loyalty club for
independent cafés. The feeling: a beautifully art-directed magazine (think
Kinfolk, Aesop, Apple's warmth) — NOT a SaaS template. Warm, human, calm,
confident, quietly witty.

PALETTE (warm analog only): cream #F3EAD7, warm cream #F7F0DE, terracotta
#D67A45, soft rose #DC8B7E, saffron #E5B14A, warm plum #8D6B8D, espresso
#1F1612. Kodak Portra film grade, subtle film grain, soft analog light
leaks. Light feels golden, like late-afternoon sun through a café window.

TYPOGRAPHY: large editorial high-contrast display serif for headlines
(à la Fraunces/Canela), tight letter-spacing; a clean modern grotesque for
body (à la Inter/Bricolage); a small uppercase mono for eyebrows and labels.
Type is a design element — big, expressive, intentional.

SIGNATURE MOTIF: a glowing gradient RING (terracotta→saffron→plum), the
brand mark. It recurs as a sun, a halo, an orbit, a portal — the hero object.

SECTION SEPARATION — critical: sections must NOT be stacked rectangles or
card grids. Separate them organically: sweeping arcs and curved dividers,
the ring's warm glow bleeding across the seam, full-bleed photography
melting into cream, asymmetric editorial layouts, and shifts in color
temperature (cream dawn → golden hour → espresso night → dawn again).
Generous negative space. Let light and color define the sections, not boxes.

IMAGERY: real, candid, warm café moments — hands passing a cup, steam in
golden light, a barista mid-smile, real faces as a circle of regulars.
Photographic, intimate, shallow depth of field. No stock cheesiness.

DO NOT INCLUDE: any blue, teal, cyan, or cold grey; dark-mode tech
aesthetic; generic SaaS dashboard rectangles; boxy card grids; glassmorphism
overload; neon; Corporate-Memphis illustrations; emoji-cluttered UI; browser
chrome. Nothing that looks like a template.
```

## Pro tips
- **Aspect ratios:** hero/sections = 16:10 or 3:2; full page = 2:3 or taller ("tall vertical artboard").
- **Iterate:** "keep the layout, make it warmer / more negative space / bigger headline."
- **Legible copy:** render sections individually; full-page renders garble small text (use for flow only).
- **Close the loop:** when you love a render, hand it back as a reference image and it gets built in performant code — exact, not approximate.
